Hybrid movie recommender

Pick a movie you like — the engine blends content similarity (genre/keyword overlap, a stand-in for TF-IDF) with a simulated collaborative-filtering signal (a stand-in for the SVD component) to rank the rest of the catalog.

Content weight (TF-IDF)
Collaborative weight (SVD)
Simplified for the browser: genre/keyword Jaccard similarity approximates TF-IDF content scoring, and a fixed per-movie popularity vector approximates the SVD collaborative signal. The full research version (see Research section) trains proper TF-IDF vectors and an SVD matrix factorization on the MovieLens dataset, evaluated with Precision@K and RMSE.
